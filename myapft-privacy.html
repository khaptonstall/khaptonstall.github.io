myAPFT Privacy Policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on myAPFT's app for iPhone.

Information We Collect

When creating a profile in myAPFT, you will be asked for your name, age, and gender. This information is collected in order to calculate Army APFT scores and is stored on your device. We never store this information on any backend service or database.

Ads and Analytics

The myAPFT app collects aggregate, anonymous statistics, such as the percentage of users who use particular features, to improve the app.

myAPFT makes use of Google's AdMob to server ads in our iPhone application. AdMob uses the devices advertising id to serve personalized ads based on the user's interests. Users can opt out of personalized ads by visiting Google's ads settings page.

Accessing, changing, or deleting your information

At any time, you may access, change, or delete the information stored on the myAPFT iPhone application by visiting the Settings tab.
